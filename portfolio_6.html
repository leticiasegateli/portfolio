About Me

I design for humans,
not screens

I'm a Senior UX/UI Designer shaping how businesses design and deliver AI experiences — turning complex systems into clear, usable, and impactful products.

Originally from the countryside of Brazil and now based on the Gold Coast, my journey shapes how I design: grounded in empathy, curiosity, and real human needs.

With a background in Business Management and a Master's in Design, I blend research, strategy, and design to turn insights into impactful, user-centred solutions.
